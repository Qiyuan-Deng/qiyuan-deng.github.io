Bio

I am currently an Assistant Professor of Information Systems at the School of Management and Economics, The Chinese University of Hong Kong, Shenzhen.

My general research agenda lies in the interface of information systems, operations, and marketing. My research uses game theory, econometrics, and data-driven decision making to address information and operational problems in businesses and society. Specific directions include e-commerce, online platforms, and social networks.

I obtained my Ph.D. in Business (Operations Management) from Lee Kong Chian School of Business, Singapore Management University, fortunately supervised by Dr. Yun Fong Lim and Dr. Xin Fang. Prior to that, I obtained my BSc in Economics (Finance) at the School of Gifted Young, University of Science and Technology of China.
